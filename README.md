# EARTH-X
Designed a solution that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through simple actions and personalized insights.
🌍 Earth X — An AI-powered carbon footprint intelligence platform that helps individuals, communities, and organizations track, visualize, and reduce their environmental impact through real-time analytics, sustainability insights, gamification, and interactive 3D Earth experiences.

Features:
• Carbon footprint tracking and analysis
• AI-driven sustainability recommendations
• Real-time environmental impact visualization
• Interactive 3D Earth simulation
• Community challenges and eco-rewards
• Progress monitoring and sustainability scoring
• Modern responsive user experience

Built to transform climate awareness into measurable action.
